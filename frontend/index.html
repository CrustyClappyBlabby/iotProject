Plant Health Calculation

The health percentage is calculated based on the status of each plant's sensors:

  • Good status: 100% contribution
  • Warning status: 50% contribution
  • Bad status: 0% contribution

The overall health is the average of all sensor readings. A score of 80-100% is considered good (green), 40-79% is a warning (yellow), and below 40% indicates poor health (red).
