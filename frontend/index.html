Plant Health Percentage - How is it calculated?
Step 1: Check 4 sensors for each plant
  • Soil Moisture
  • Temperature
  • Light Intensity
  • Air Humidity
Step 2: Give points to each sensor
  • Optimal range (100 points): Optimal conditions for the plant
  • Warning range (50 points): Not ideal, but acceptable
  • Critical range (0 points): Bad conditions for the plant
Step 3: Calculate average

Plant Health = (Points from all 4 sensors) √∑ 4
